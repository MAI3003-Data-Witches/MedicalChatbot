# MedicalChatbot

## Objective
This bot should help COPD and asthma patients discern which of their symptoms is worrying and could be an exacerbation.
Chatbot that gives calming exercises during an episode/attack/whatever and refers to helpful resources
Describing drug information: side effects, general use, any drug interactions?

## Population
COPD and asthma patients

## Patient needs
- Good communication
- Empathy
